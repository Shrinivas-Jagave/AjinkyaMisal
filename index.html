About Us

Ajinkya Misal is a place where tradition, taste, and quality come together. We proudly serve authentic Maharashtrian misal prepared using fresh ingredients, traditional spices, and time-tested recipes. Our goal is to give every customer the true flavor of homemade misal with rich tarri, crispy farsan, and perfectly balanced spices.

We believe good food creates good memories. Visit Ajinkya Misal and enjoy the authentic taste of Maharashtra that will keep you coming back again.
